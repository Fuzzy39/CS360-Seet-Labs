Settings

Manage all of your session settings here.

Copy Session Key

The session key contains all of your progress. It is not gauranteed that this progress will be saved the next time you visit. However, having a copy of your key will allow you to retain your session on every visit.



Reset Progress

All your progress will be reset. Be careful and save a backup copy of your key if you want to save your progress.



Update Key

Paste a backup key to restore your progress from a previous session.
